1. 规律
   `1/(1-2x)` <=> `f(n) = 2f(**n**-1)`
   `1/(1-x-x^2)` <=> `f(n) = f(n-1) + f(n-2)`
   `1-a*x-b*x^2+...+` <=> `f(n) = a*f(n-1) + b*f(n-2) + ...`

   - 母函数除以(1-x),就相当于取前缀和
   - `1/(1-x)^n` <=> `∑C(n+k-1,k)*x^k` (k>=0)
